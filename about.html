About CardResell

Independent · Built by resellers, for resellers

CardResell is a lightweight tool that helps trading-card sellers answer one question fast: where do I actually make the most money on this card, after fees?

Every marketplace advertises "low fees." In practice, eBay, Mercari, Poshmark, StockX, and COMC each take a different cut, add different shipping quirks, and pay out at different speeds. We pull the real numbers together in one place so you can list where the payout is highest — not where the marketing is loudest.

What we do

  • Card Lookup — Search any Pokémon, Magic, or Yu-Gi-Oh! card and see live sold comps (English + Japanese Pokémon).
  • Fee breakdown — Same card, every marketplace, side by side. Actual net payout — not "list price."
  • Estimated Grade Scan — Photo a raw card and get an in-app centering / corners / edges / surface estimate before you drop $30+ on PSA/BGS.
  • Sales Tracker — Log what you bought, what you sold it for, and where. Watch your realized P&L instead of guessing.

How we make money

CardResell Pro is $9.99/month for the full fee breakdown, unlimited flip tracking, and 10 free Grade Scans a month. Card Lookup packs are $1–$9 pay-as-you-go and never expire. When you click through to an eBay listing we may earn an affiliate credit from eBay Partner Network — it never changes the price you pay.

What we’re not

CardResell is not a grading company, not a marketplace, and not affiliated with The Pokémon Company, Nintendo, PSA, BGS, CGC, or SGC. Prices and fees are estimates — always verify with the actual marketplace before you list.
