CardResell
Built by a card flipper, for card flippers
CardResell was built by Will Jones in Bradenton, Florida to solve a problem every reseller hits — you know your card is worth money but you have no idea which platform actually pays you the most after fees.

The app pulls live sold comps from eBay and real-time prices from the TCG market, then calculates your exact take-home on every major platform side by side — eBay, COMC, Poshmark, Fanatics Collect, and PWCC — so you never leave money on the table.

The AI Grading Estimate uses GPT-4o vision to analyze centering, corners, edges, and surface — giving you a realistic grade prediction before you spend money on a PSA submission.
