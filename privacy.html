Last updated: July 1, 2026

This policy explains what CardResell (cardresell.org, "we") collects, why we collect it, and your rights. We try to collect as little as possible.

2. What we don't collect

  • We don't ask for your phone number, address, or date of birth.
  • We don't sell your data to advertisers or data brokers.
  • We don't use your card photos to train third-party AI models.

3. Third-party services

  • Google Sign-In — authentication (Google privacy policy)
  • Stripe — payments; card details never touch our servers (Stripe privacy policy)
  • Vercel — hosting & edge functions (Vercel privacy policy)
  • OpenAI / Anthropic — the models that power ID Scan and Grade Scan. Photos are sent for inference only; providers are contractually forbidden from training on our data.
  • eBay Partner Network — affiliate tracking on outbound eBay links.

4. Your rights

You can:

  • Export your Sales Tracker data from the settings panel any time.
  • Delete your account — email legal@cardresell.org and we'll erase everything within 30 days (except records we're legally required to retain, like purchase receipts).
  • Access, correct, or restrict processing under GDPR / CCPA — same email.
  • Opt out of analytics — use a browser that blocks trackers, or email us.

5. Cookies

We use a session cookie to keep you signed in and a preference cookie to remember your theme and dismissed banners. That's it — no advertising cookies.

6. Children

CardResell is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, email legal@cardresell.org and we'll delete it.

7. Changes

We'll post material changes on this page and email account holders when they affect your rights.

Contact

Privacy questions or data requests — legal@cardresell.org.
